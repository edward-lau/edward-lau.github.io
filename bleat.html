Bleat

A game made for Audiogame Jam 2. The game was designed to be playable without sight, relying entirely on audio to provide player feedback.

The goal is to reunite four sheep scattered across an island. Locate them by listening, and herd them back together.

Edward Lau (Design, code, dialogue & sfx)
Made with Unity

Implementation Details

  • Audio implementation using Wwise.
  • Sheep emit different vocals at different intervals depending on whether they're being herded or not.
  • Surface dependent footsteps so the player knows they've reached the beach at the edge of the island.
  • Audio emitters that follow the player around the edge of the island playing ocean sounds.
  • Random environment sounds (plant rustling and seagulls) using user-defined random positioning.
